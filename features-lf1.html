Features - Load Factor One

Control what matters

M;T3R's demand-response solution focuses on the largest energy consumer of a 21st century household, the electric car. With Load Factor One it is possible to optimise charging time and speed of multiple charging points within an area, and shift various EV charging needs from peak to valley periods.

Create clear incentives

Our hardware solution tracks charging sessions and makes it possible to create area-based, group-level, or even personal price incentives. Price signals can be delivered via the M;T3R app.

Flexible charging

M;T3R can identify cars in the fleet, even when charging happens at the home of the user. This solution feature coupled with our invoicing service lets companies avoid or postpone considerable investments in on-site charging facilities.
