Features - MET3R³

Track consumption in real-time

With M;T3R³ anyone can track his current consumption in real-time and have access to historical data with the tap of the finger.

Achieve maximum savings

Personalised saving tips, real-time feedback on the customer's actions and predictive analysis enables maximum savings.

Set targets or compare with similar households

Customers can set weekly or monthly energy-saving targets and see their progress in real-time. The app enables comparison with similar households’ consumption.

App or device?

The M;T3R app in itself allows for logging and interpreting the consumption on an ad-hoc basis. Customers can use the smartphone's camera to record the meter reading. If you decide to install the inexpensive and non-invasive M;T3R device, a simple, but powerful smart metering tool, then data recording is continuous, real-time and granule to identify individual appliances.

Easy installation

Installing the M;T3R³ is safe, quick and easy. Anyone can perform installation and set up the system within minutes, there is no need for intervention by a professional.

Top class privacy

M;T3R offers 21st century privacy-protection. Using smart home devices results in a huge amount of sensitive personal data. Data is encrypted end-to-end and stored in a blockchain-based database. This data belongs to you and with M;T3R only you are empowered to access and share it with others.

Enjoy security & peace of mind

M;T3R sends you real-time alerts on your phone if an appliance has broken down, left on unintentionally (kettle), or if there is unusual activity in your home while you are away (burglary or energy theft).

Streamline relations with your customers

With M;T3R energy customers can verify bills, identify the tariffs best fitting their situation, or set up notifications to stay within pre-set limits. Periodic meter readings can be reported automatically or with minimal customer action, with a single click.
